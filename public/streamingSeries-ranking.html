The Best Streaming Series According to Alison

My top 3 series so far in 2024/ 2025!

Severance

Something original and new in the sci-fi genre, engaging and thought provoking.

For All Mankind

An alt-history story where the Russians beat NASA to the moon; unexpectedly incredible drama set in the space-fi genre.

Foundation

Leave Earth and experience intergalactic drama and high stakes, incredible tech, new worlds, beliefs and cultures among the stars.
